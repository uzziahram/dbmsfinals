# LiMS (Library Management System) - User Manual

Welcome to **LiMS**, a modern, web-based Library Management System designed to bridge the gap between digital and physical reading. This manual provides a comprehensive guide for both regular members and administrators.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Authentication
*   **Registration:** New users must create an account via the **Register** page. You will need to provide your full name, a unique username, a valid email address, and your physical address.
*   **Login:** Existing members can access their dashboard by entering their registered email and password on the **Login** page.

### 1.2 The Dashboard
Upon logging in, you are greeted with your personalized dashboard.
*   **Book Shelves:** Browse the entire library collection categorized by availability.
*   **Search Bar:** Quickly find books by Title, Author, or Genre.
*   **Profile Link:** Access your transaction history and manage your account.

---

## 2. Browsing & Discovering

### 2.1 Searching
Use the search bar at the top of the homepage. The system performs a real-time search across:
*   **Book Titles**
*   **Author Names**
*   **Genre Tags** (e.g., "Philosophy", "Fiction")

### 2.2 Filtering by Genre
Use the Genre Filter to narrow down your search. You can select multiple genres to find books that match all selected criteria.

---

## 3. Transactions

### 3.1 Borrowing Books
LiMS supports two formats for borrowing:
*   **Softcopy (E-Books):** Instant access upon borrowing. You can read these directly from your profile.
*   **Hardcopy (Physical Books):** Subject to stock availability. Borrowing a hardcopy creates a "Pending" request for you to pick up at the library.

### 3.2 Purchasing Books
If you wish to own a copy, you can use the **Purchase** option.
*   Select your preferred format (if available).
*   Specify the quantity.
*   Complete the transaction using the integrated payment simulation.

---

## 4. Managing Your Profile

Access your profile by clicking the **Profile** button on the dashboard.

### 4.1 Borrowed Books
*   View all books you currently have on loan.
*   **Returning Softcopies:** You can click the "Return Book" button to instantly return digital copies.
*   **Returning Hardcopies:** In accordance with our safety policy, **hardcopies must be returned physically** to the librarian. Your profile will display "Physical Return Required" for these items.

### 4.2 Purchased Books
View a permanent list of all books you have bought, including the purchase date and format.

### 4.3 Unified Activity History
The **Activity History** section provides a chronological log of all your interactions with the library, whether it's a new borrow, a return, or a purchase.

---

## 5. Administrative Notes (For Staff)

*   **Stock Management:** Hardcopy stock is automatically decremented upon borrow/purchase and incremented upon a physical return being logged by the staff.
*   **Return Verification:** Staff must use the administrative dashboard to verify the condition of physical returns before updating the status in the system.

---

## 6. Troubleshooting & FAQ

*   **"Insufficient Stock":** This occurs when all physical copies are currently loaned out. You may still borrow the softcopy version if available.
*   **Login Issues:** Ensure your email is correct. If you forget your password, please contact the library administrator.
*   **Overdue Books:** Please return physical books by the due date displayed in your profile to avoid account restrictions.
